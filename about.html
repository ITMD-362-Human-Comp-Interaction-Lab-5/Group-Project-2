About Plenty of Fish

Founded in 2003, Plenty of Fish (POF) is one of the world's largest online dating platforms. Our mission is to connect singles and help them build meaningful relationships. With a user-friendly interface and a commitment to inclusivity, POF has brought millions of people together from around the globe.

Our Mission

At Plenty of Fish, we believe everyone deserves to find their perfect match. We are dedicated to creating a safe, inclusive, and enjoyable platform where people can connect, communicate, and grow their relationships.
